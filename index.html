Sanrio Characters

Hello Kitty

Hello Kitty, whose real name is Kitty White, is a cheerful, kind-hearted British girl (not a cat, but a human in a cat motif) from London, known for her simple design with black eyes, a yellow nose, and a signature red bow on her left ear, symbolizing the kawaii (cute) culture with a heartwarming lack of a mouth, allowing people to project their own feelings onto her face as she "speaks from the heart". She loves baking, playing the piano, collecting cute things, and is a global icon for Sanrio, appearing on countless products and media.

My Melody

My Melody is a kind, honest, and sweet white rabbit from Sanrio, known for wearing a pink (originally red) hood inspired by Little Red Riding Hood, living in Mariland, and loving almond pound cake. She's a cheerful, slightly naive character who enjoys baking and helping others, but sometimes offers blunt advice, and is best friends with My Sweet Piano, though she's often depicted as rivals with Kuromi, whom she considers a friend.

My Sweet Piano

My Sweet Piano is a gentle, pink sheep from Sanrio, best friends with My Melody, known for her soft baby-pink wool, love of meringue cookies, and enjoying flower-picking and playing the piano; she debuted in 1980, communicates through sheep noises in some series but speaks in others, and has gained popularity for her sweet, slightly pampered, and creative personality, appearing in anime, games, and themed merchandise.
